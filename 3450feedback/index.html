COMP 3450 Course Feedback

Thank you for your interest in showing your support for improvements to COMP 3450: Human Computer Interaction.

The club has removed its letter to the department from its website, as concerns are being addressed by the department chair and deans of science. If you're a student that added their support and expressed their concerns, hats off for demanding attention to instructional and course quality in Computing Science at TRU - we're continuing to demonstrate that changes are needed in our program, with the help of our department and the faculty of science.

Get Involved

If you have concerns about or feedback to improve COMP 3450 and other Computing Science courses at TRU, please contact the appropriate faculty member or the department chair . If you have questions about the correct way to raise your concerns so that they are heard and acted upon, please contact the club.
