Support Lab

The Computer Science Club runs a semesterly support lab to help students needing some extra help and guidance in lower level classes with their assignments and topics. All the hours, and the current status, of the support lab can be found on this page.

Winter 2015

The support lab is still being prepared for the semester, and should be up and running shortly. Meanwhile, send us an email, or make an appointment with your instructor during their office hours. A schedule for the lab should be available here soon!

Want to find out when we open? Register for our mailing list to the left of the page!

Know your stuff? Volunteer with the Support Lab!

The winter 2015 call for volunteers is now open! Each semester, students depend on the support lab for help with lower level courses. The entire operation is volunteer run, so we need students like you to help out! If you're a strong computing student willing to share your knowledge, fill out the form below to get started!
